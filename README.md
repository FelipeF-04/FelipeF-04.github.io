# 💪 GymMe - AI-Powered Social Fitness Platform

> **[🚀 Try the Live Demo](https://felipef-04.github.io/)**

A modern social fitness app built with **Flutter** that combines community engagement with AI-powered exercise form analysis. Share your workouts, get intelligent form feedback, and connect with fitness enthusiasts worldwide.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎥 **Video Sharing & Analysis**
- **Video Upload** - Compress and upload workout videos with custom thumbnails
- **AI Exercise Grading** - Advanced form analysis for squats, bench press, and deadlifts with real-time feedback on technique
- **Video Search** - Discover trending workouts and fitness content from the community

### 🤝 **Community & Engagement**
- **User Profiles** - Share your profile pictures, workout history, and training stats
- **Real-time Comments** - Engage with other users through instant commenting
- **Connect & Follow** - Build your fitness network with connection requests and followers

### 🧮 **Training Tools**
- **RM Calculator** - Accurately estimate your one-rep max based on your lifts
- **Set Comparison** - Scientifically compare sets to determine better protocols for strength vs. hypertrophy
- **Workout Notebook** - Comprehensive session tracking and training log
- **Calendar Integration** - Plan and track your gym sessions over time

### 🎮 **Advanced Features**
- **Betting System** - Participate in fitness challenge pools with friends
- **Offline Functionality** - Full app access without internet connectivity

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Open the App** - Visit the [live demo](https://felipef-04.github.io/)
2. **Create Account** - Sign up via the login screen
3. **Explore**:
   - Upload a workout video and experience the AI grading system
   - Search for test user profile: `test1234` and send a connection request
   - Try the RM Calculator, Set Comparison tool, and Workout Notebook
   - Schedule workouts using the calendar feature
